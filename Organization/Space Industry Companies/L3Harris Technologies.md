**Name**: L3Harris Technologies
**Website**: https://www.l3harris.com/

>[!summary]
>L3Harris Technologies is a leading aerospace and defense company specializing in advanced communications, electronic systems, and mission-critical technologies for government and commercial customers. Formed through the merger of L3 Technologies and Harris Corporation in 2019, the company focuses on solutions for space, air, land, and sea domains, with a significant presence in space systems.

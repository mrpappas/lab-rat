## Partner Details

Website: https://www.gov.uk/government/organisations/ministry-of-defence

The UK Ministry of Defence (MOD) is a UK government department responsible for national security, defence policy, and overseeing the British Armed Forces (Royal Navy, Army, RAF) to protect the UK, its people, and interests at home and abroad, working with allies to ensure global peace and stability, managing budgets, equipment, and personnel, and serving as the central body for defence strategy and operations.

This satellite joins the rest of the QPS-SAR constellation in providing high resolution synthetic aperture radar (SAR) images and Earth monitoring services globally. iQPS aims to build a constellation of 36 SAR satellites that will provide near-real-time images of Earth every 10 minutes.

"The Nation God Navigates" was the sixth mission deployed on Electron for iQPS, making Rocket Lab the go-to provider for their constellation.

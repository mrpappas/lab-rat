## Summary

Heimdall is a Space Domain Awareness solution that incorporates advanced Electro-Optical sensors for on-orbit sensing operations.
 - High-resolution imaging with real-time algorithmic processing
 - Low-size weight and power (SWaP), compact payloads for scalable constellation integreation
 - Full EO solutions, from space-based sensors to ground-based data exploitation
 - Affordable and purpose-built for national security space architecture

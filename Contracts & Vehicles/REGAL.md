## Activity

### CRADA with [[USTRANSCOM]]
6 Sep 2022

Rocket Lab signed a Cooperative Research and Development Agreement with [[USTRANSCOM]] to explore using **[[Neutron]]**, **[[Electron]]**, and **[[Photon]]** to transport cargo globally.

- Investigated on-orbit **[[Photon]] depots** for cargo staging and re-entry
- Modeled logistics timelines, G-force constraints, and delivery architectures
- Supported early requirements development for the **Rocket Cargo Vanguard**

üîó [Press Release](https://www.rocketlabusa.com/updates/rocket-lab-signs-agreement-with-ustranscom-to-explore-using-neutron-and-electron-rockets-to-deliver-cargo-around-the-world/)

> *This CRADA was non-monetary, but paved the way for future flight demos under REGAL.*

### REGAL Call 002 Task Order: Neutron Return-to-Earth Demo
8 May 2025

Rocket Lab was awarded a task order under REGAL Call 002 to conduct a full **return-to-Earth Neutron mission** for cargo survivability testing.

- Launches a mass-representative payload aboard **Neutron**
- Performs controlled re-entry and landing with full cargo intact
- Evaluates TPS, dynamic loads, landing accuracy, and rapid offload

## Activity

### Initial Contract Award - 10 Oct 2019
Rocket Lab‚Äôs **Electron** was one of eight vehicles on the original OSP-4 award, making the company eligible to compete for every task order.
### [[STP-S30]] Task Order - 8 Apr 2024
SSC awarded Rocket Lab a **$14.49 M** launch-service task order to fly **[[STP-S30]]** from LC-2 (Wallops, VA). The flight will carry the [DISKSat VLEO demonstrator](https://www.nasa.gov/smallspacecraft/disksat/) and other DoD experiments NET 2026.
### Path to Medium-Class Missions
Rocket Lab has signaled that **Neutron** will be offered on future OSP-4 on-ramps, expanding its coverage to payloads up to ~13 t (reusable).

## Launch-Readiness Timeline

OSP-4 task orders require providers to deliver spacecraft **within 12-24 months** of award; timelines can be compressed further for TacRS needs.
